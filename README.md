#🎬 Amazon Prime Video Content Analysis Dashboard

## 📊 Project Objective
To analyze Amazon Prime’s global content library and uncover insights about movie and TV show distribution by genre, rating, country, and release year.

## 🧩 Dataset Used
**File Name:** `amazon_prime_titles.xlsx`
**Size:** 9,668 titles
**Columns:** Title, Type, Release Year, Genre, Director, Country, Rating, Duration

## 🧮 Key Metrics
- **Total Titles:** 9,668
- **Total Ratings:** 25
- **Total Genres:** 518
- **Total Directors:** 5,769

## 📈 Dashboard Insights
- **Genre Trends:** Drama and Comedy are the most frequent genres.
- **Content Growth:** Rapid increase in new releases after 2010.
- **Country Distribution:** USA, India, and the UK lead in total content.
- **Ratings:** TV-MA and TV-14 are the most common.
- **Type Split:** Movies (80%) vs TV Shows (20%).

## 🛠️ Tools Used
- **Power BI** (Data Visualization)
- **Power Query** (Data Cleaning)
- **Excel** (Dataset Preparation)
- **DAX** (Custom Measures)

## 💡 Business Impact
Helps content strategists identify production trends, target regions, and audience preferences across different categories.

## 🏁 Outcome
Built an interactive dashboard summarizing Amazon Prime’s content portfolio and trends, enabling better content strategy and business decisions.




# 👥 HR Analytics Dashboard

## 🎯 Project Objective
To identify employee attrition patterns and understand key factors like age, gender, department, and education field influencing workforce retention.

## 🧩 Dataset Used
**File Name:** `HR_Data.xlsx`
**Records:** 1,470 employees
**Fields:** Age, Gender, Department, Job Role, Education, Job Satisfaction, Attrition

## 🧮 Key Metrics
- **Overall Employees:** 1,470
- **Active Employees:** 1,233
- **Attrition:** 237
- **Attrition Rate:** 16.12%
- **Average Age:** 37 years

## 📊 Dashboard Insights
- **Highest Attrition:** R&D Department (54.12%)
- **Education Impact:** Life Sciences and Medical fields show higher attrition.
- **Age Distribution:** Majority of attrition occurs in the 25–34 age group.
- **Gender Ratio:** Balanced workforce with slightly higher attrition among males.
- **Job Satisfaction:** Visible correlation between lower satisfaction and higher attrition.

## 🛠️ Tools Used
- **Power BI** (Visualization)
- **DAX** (KPIs & Measures)
- **Power Query** (Data Cleaning)
- **Excel** (Pre-processing)

## 💡 Business Impact
Helps HR departments identify high-risk groups, improve employee engagement, and reduce attrition through data-backed decisions.

## 🏁 Outcome
Delivered an analytical dashboard that provides actionable insights into employee retention, demographics, and job satisfaction.

**File Name:** `Vrinda_Store_Data.xlsx`
**Fields:** Order Date, Gender, State, Channel, Category, Amount, Age Group

- **Top Channels:** Amazon (35.5%), Flipkart (23.4%), Meesho (21.6%)

- Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, Conditional Formatting
- Interactive Slicers for filtering by state, month, and category

## 💡 Business Impact
Supports management in identifying top-performing states, customer demographics, and best-performing sales channels.

Built an interactive Excel dashboard summarizing Vrinda Store’s annual sales and performance for data-driven marketing and inventory planning.


# 🎬 Amazon Prime Video Dashboard (Power BI Project)

## 🎯 Project Objective
To analyze and visualize Amazon Prime’s global content library to understand trends in genres, release years, countries, and audience ratings.  
The goal was to uncover insights into **content distribution patterns** and **audience targeting** to support data-driven content strategies.

---

## 🧩 Dataset Used
**File Name:** `Amazon_Prime.xlsx`  
**Source:** Publicly available dataset on Amazon Prime titles  
**Rows:** ~9,600 records  
**Columns:** Title, Type, Country, Release Year, Rating, Duration, Genre, Director, Cast

---

## 🧮 Key Metrics
- **Total Titles:** 9,668  
- **Movies:** 7,841  
- **TV Shows:** 1,827  
- **Unique Genres:** 518  
- **Directors:** 5,769  
- **Ratings Categories:** 25  

---

## 📊 Dashboard Insights
- **Genre Distribution:** *Drama* and *Comedy* are the most common genres on the platform.  
- **Content Type:** 80% Movies, 20% TV Shows — showing Amazon’s focus on movies.  
- **Top Countries:** USA, India, and UK lead in content production.  
- **Ratings Breakdown:** Majority of content is rated **TV-MA** (Mature) and **TV-14**, showing a focus on adult and teenage audiences.  
- **Release Year Trend:** Sharp content growth after 2015, aligning with Amazon’s global market expansion.  
- **Director Contributions:** Steven Spielberg and Woody Allen among top contributors.

---

## 📈 Key Visuals in Dashboard
- 📊 Bar Chart – Top 10 Genres by Count  
- 🌍 Map Visualization – Titles by Country  
- 📆 Line Chart – Year-wise Release Trend  
- 🎥 Donut Chart – Movies vs TV Shows Ratio  
- ⭐ Clustered Bar Chart – Ratings Distribution  
- 🔎 Cards & KPIs – Total Titles, Genres, Ratings  

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Techniques Used
- **Power BI Desktop** – Data Modeling & Visualization  
- **Power Query** – Data Cleaning & Transformation  
- **DAX Functions** – Calculated Measures (e.g., Count of Titles, % Movies vs TV Shows)  
- **Excel** – Initial Data Review and Cleaning  

---

## 💡 Business Impact
- Provided a clear view of **content diversity and geographic focus**.  
- Helped understand **audience segmentation** based on rating and genre.  
- Insights can assist streaming strategists in **content planning** and **regional marketing** decisions.  

---

## 🏁 Outcome
Built an **interactive and user-friendly Power BI dashboard** that summarizes Amazon Prime’s entire content library.  
The report helps identify trends in content type, geography, and audience ratings, empowering business teams to make **data-driven content acquisition and marketing decisions**.
